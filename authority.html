After becoming a leader, you can do the following actions:

Clan Chief

  • Spend 5GMT to change the clan name

  • Initiate clan adventures

Guild Master

  • Spend 10GMT to change the guild name

  • Initiate guild adventures

  • Expel non-officer users from the guild (requires 1 move base item, inactive users do not require)

District Rep

District Rep

  • Spend 20GMT to change the district name

City Mayor

  • Spend 100GMT to expand a new district (existing district population reaches 85% or more, and the number of districts in the city cannot exceed 40)

World Elders

  • Confidential for now
