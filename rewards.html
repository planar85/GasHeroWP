Rewards

Source

The source of the rewards is the donation from the Elder's selection in the previous election cycle and the revenue from various auction houses. The time periods for auction house revenue are described as follows.

Individual Match:

The auction house revenue from the 30th day of the previous election cycle to the 9th day of the current election cycle (a total of 12 days).

Clan Match:

The auction house revenue from the 10th day to the 19th day of the current election cycle (a total of 10 days).

Guild Match:

The auction house revenue from the 20th day to the 29th day of the current election cycle (a total of 10 days).

Summary:

All World Elders’ donation funds and half of the auction sales revenue will be used as rewards, while the remaining auction sales revenue will be official revenue.

Reward

Specifications

Individual Match

  • Clan Champion Rewards: 2.5% of the total revenue of your own guild's auction house during the 30th day of the previous election cycle to the 9th day of the current election cycle.

  • Guild Champion Rewards: 27.5% of the total revenue of your own guild's auction house during the 30th day of the previous election cycle to the 9th day of the current election cycle.

  • District Champion Rewards: 25% of the total revenue of the auction house in your own district during the 30th day of the previous election cycle to the 9th day of the current election cycle.

  • City Champion Rewards: 20% of the total revenue of all districts' auction houses in your own city during the 30th day of the previous election cycle to the 9th day of the current election cycle.

  • World Champion Rewards: 5% of the total revenue of all districts' auction houses worldwide during the 30th day of the previous election cycle to the 9th day of the current election cycle, plus 30% of the donation from the Elder's selection in the previous election cycle.

Clan Match

  • Guild Champion Rewards: 50% of the total revenue of your own guild's auction house during the 10th day to the 19th day of the current election cycle.

  • District Champion Rewards: 20% of the total revenue of the auction house in your own district during the 10th day to the 19th day of the current election cycle.

  • City Champion Rewards: 20% of the total revenue of all districts' auction houses in your own city during the 10th day to the 19th day of the current election cycle.

  • World Champion Rewards: 10% of the total revenue of all districts' auction houses worldwide during the 10th day to the 19th day of the current election cycle, plus 30% of the donation from the Elder's selection in the previous election cycle.

Guild match

  • District Champion Rewards: 50% of the total revenue of guilds' auction house in your own district during the 20th day to the 29th day of the current election cycle, plus 15% of the total revenue of the auction house in your own district.

  • City Champion Rewards: 20% of the total revenue of all districts' auction houses in your own city during the 20th day to the 29th day of the current election cycle.

  • World Champion Rewards: 15% of the total revenue of all districts' auction houses worldwide during the 20th day to the 29th day of the current election cycle, plus 40% of the donation from the Elder's selection in the previous election cycle.

Claiming

  • After the event is over, champions from each level will receive mails to claim their corresponding rewards.

  • Individual match rewards will be claimed by individuals.

  • Clan match rewards will be claimed by clan chiefs.

  • Guild match rewards will be claimed by guild masters.
