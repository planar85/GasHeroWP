Operation Mode

Operation Mode

Gas Hero uses an automatic combat mode. Players need to configure their own combat lineup in advance and use it to fight in various battles.

Lineup

Lineup

The lineup consists of a total of 6 heroes. Only one hero with each codename can be included in your lineup. Each hero can equip a weapon and carry a pet.

Action Rules

Action Rules

Next to each hero is a vertical action bar. When the bar is full, the hero will attack or use a hero skill if available. The recharge rate is related to the hero's speed attribute.

Below each hero is a horizontal rage bar, which increases when the hero acts or is attacked. When the bar is full, the hero's action bar recharge rate will double for 15 seconds.

Standard Attack

Standard Attack

Your hero's standard attack is determined by their weapon type.

Hero Skills

Hero Skills

Each hero can have up to seven skills. Some require MP to be used, some strengthen the hero's attributes. More details will be provided in the next chapter.

Basic Attributes

Basic Attributes

The five primary attributes in this game are Attack, Defence, Health Points (HP), Magic Points(MP), and Speed. When calculating damage from an attack, subtract the target's Defence value from the attacker's Attack value. Damage mitigation is capped at 20% of the attacker's Attack value. Skills can amplify damage, sometimes consume MP. Additionally, a character's Speed determines the rate at which their action bar increases.
