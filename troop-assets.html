Genesis Hero probability:
unminted quantity / 20000 * 100%

Hero Upgrade

Hero Upgrade

The maximum level of heroes of different rarities is different. Each level up requires the use of Hero Potion, and when the level is a multiple of 10, additional GMT is required.

Leveling up can increase the hero's basic attributes: Attack, Defense, and HP. MP and Speed will not increase with level. How much each level increases depends on the hero's attribute growth rate, which is randomly determined when the hero is born.

Breeding

Breeding

Breeding is an important way to obtain new heroes, and the following conditions must be met:

  1. Parents need to be one male and one female.

  2. Parents must reach level 10.

  3. Each Parent’s breed times is less than 7.

  4. Each hero can breed once every 3 days.

The codename of the new hero is influenced by parents, and is most likely to be the same as the parents’, with a small chance of changing to another codename.

Weapon Reforging/ Recycling

Weapon Reforging/
Recycling

When the attributes of the weapon you obtain are not satisfactory, you can use Reforging Dust for reforging:

  1. The types and amount of attributes of the weapon will not change.

  2. The values of all attributes of the weapon will be randomly reshuffled within a certain range.

  3. When the number of consecutive reforgings reaches a certain number, the lower limit of the random value range will be increased, and this can happen multiple times.

  1. If there is no reforging for 1 hour, the accumulated number of reforgings will be reset to 0.

  2. Selling will result in the accumulated number of reforgings being reset to 0.

  3. Reforging high-quality weapons requires the consumption of GMT.

Excess weapons can also be recycled into reforging dust.
