Attaining Heroes

The total number of Genesis Heroes will not exceed 2084. The design of the Genesis Heroes will come from the following sources:

  1. Before the game goes live, the game will select 960 heroes from all three phases of the Genesis Hero Design Contest.

  2. The remaining 1124 heroes’ designs will be gradually filled from partnerships and IP collaborations.

How to obtain heroes:

  1. Genesis Heroes - outside of the game: obtained through various community events and activities.

  2. Genesis Heroes - inside the game: 500 Genesis Heroes can be dropped through Hero Capsules with a low probability. Hero Capsules are available in various in-game auction houses.

  3. Heroes - outside of the game: obtained through various community activities.

  4. Heroes - inside the game: obtained through Hero Capsules, and DNA Fragments synthesis.

Locking and usage principles:

  1. When the hero NFT is in the user’s wallet, it needs to be locked before it can be used in the game.

  2. The hero can be unlocked and put up for sale, or it can simply be unlocked.

NFT minting:

  1. When the hero is born, the Hero NFT must be minted and locked in the user's wallet before being used.

Attaining Weapons + Pets

Attaining
Weapons + Pets

Ways to obtain:

  1. Outside the game: obtained through various community activities.

  2. Inside the game: obtained through blueprint fragments and ancient fragments from adventures and auction houses.

Locking and usage rules are the same as for heroes.

NFT minting:

  1. Weapons and pets are only minted as on chain NFTs when they need to be put up for sale in order to minimize the costs to the user.

Attaining Materials + Items

Attaining
Materials + Items

Refers to DNA fragments, blueprint fragments, ancient fragments, hero potion, reforging dust, evolution cookie, power can, base construction vehicle, container, etc.

Ways to obtain:

  1. Outside the game: some are obtained through various community activities.

  2. Inside the game: some are obtained through adventures, or directly obtained in the auction houses.

NFT minting and consuming:

  1. To optimize costs for the user, materials and items are minted as NFTs on the chain only when they are to be listed for sale. Different materials come with various package quantity options, like 100 hero potion being bundled into 1 hero potion package.

  2. When material package NFTs are burned, the corresponding resources are credited to the game account for in-game use.
